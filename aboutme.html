About me

I draw my inspiration mostly from nature and art. I like to look at what other artists draw, write, paint etc. and get inspired from them. I like to see their creation come to life as well, whether that’s by playing a video game that has a 3D implementation of the art or by watching a movie that recreates a story.

I’m passionate about using technology to bring ideas to life. One way of doing that is by generating photo-realistic images to visualize how worlds, landscapes, characters, creatures, buildings, etc. would look like if they existed in the real world without actually having them exist.
